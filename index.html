Action movies

John Wick

The story of the film revolves around a former professional killer who returns after killing his wife and stealing his car, and John continues to confront challengers who are trying to eliminate him

The Dark Knight

The film's story revolves around Batman and his confrontation with the villain, the Joker, and the film deals with themes of chaos and justice in an exciting way

The Maze Runner

The story of the film revolves around a young man who wakes up in a maze with no memory. He and a group of young men must solve the puzzle of the maze to find out.

Drama movies

The Godfather

The film revolves around the American Corleone crime family, focusing on the youngest son, Michael, who is implicitly forced to succeed his father.

The Shawshank Redemption

The film's story revolves around Indiana, who is convicted of a murder he did not commit and is sentenced to life imprisonment. He tries to escape with a colleague.

Titanic

The story of the film revolves around an interesting fictional love story between Jack, a poor young man, and Rose, a rich girl, on board the famous ship Titanic.

Science fiction movies

The Matrix

The film's story revolves around a virtual world called "The Matrix," where the main character, "Neo," finds himself in a struggle between truth and illusion. The film presents a complex and exciting story.

Interstellar

The film's events revolve around a team of astronauts who travel through outer space in search of new planets that can be colonized by an endangered humanity, and it combines science and love.

Inception

The film's story revolves around stealing ideas by indulging in dreams of modernization. It is one of the best and most famous science fiction films because of its complex and exciting story.
